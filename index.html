Shahan Shahid Nawaz

I am an aspiring data journalist passionate about storytelling with experience leading data analysis in multiple contexts including development economics, urban science, sports, and philanthropy research.

Currently, I am pursuing the Master of Information Management Systems program at UC Berkeley's School of Information through which I am studying data science, tech policy, and data visualization. Aiming to build a career in data journalism, I am serving as a Projects Developer for The Daily Californian, the campus' newspaper. In the past, I helped implement a randomized controlled trial in Pakistan aimed at mending the citizen-state social contract; and was based in Hong Kong where I served as the Centre for Asian Philanthropy's main data resource and contributed to reports and newsletters.
